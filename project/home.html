Key Aspects of the Indian Constitution

Justice

The Constitution ensures social, economic, and political justice for all citizens.

Liberty

It guarantees individual freedom through fundamental rights like speech and expression.

Equality

Equality of status and opportunity is enshrined in the Constitution to ensure fair treatment fxor all.

Fraternity

Promoting unity and brotherhood across all individuals to preserve the dignity of every citizen.
